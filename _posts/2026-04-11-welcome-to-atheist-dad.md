Welcome to **atheist.dad**. This blog is a place for writing about life as an atheist, and how that identity touches on technology, art, science, opinion, and culture.

The first posts will focus on:

- how secular values shape daily thinking
- the role of science and skepticism in personal decisions
- creativity through photography, drawing, and painting
- technology and culture from a humanist perspective
- opinion pieces on social issues and ethical questions

This site is built with Jekyll and Markdown so new posts stay easy to create. The plan is to keep the blog focused, thoughtful, and easy to maintain.
